ABOUT US

Cafe Louie opened in 2017 at A-M Road, Bypass junction, kothamangalam. We strive to provide the best experience to our guests. Cafe Louie is where people often meet people, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee. In 2019, Cafe Louie expanded as a cafe and restaurant which offers wide verity of cafe and restaurant items.We are the first one introduced charcol shawarma to this city. We took franchise from CCD(Cafe Coffee Day), Baskin Robbins and Meriiboy. Our brotherhood in the restaurant sector is Zaawiah. Now cafe Louie is becoming the taste of the city. We are now ready to give franchise.
